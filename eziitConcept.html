How We Are Able To Help

The high interest rates on short term loans are esssentially because of the high risk involved in transacting between Individuals, Employers, Merchants and Financial Institutions. Eziit creates an environment in which this risk is eliminated and/or reduced through establishing Trust, enabling Collaborataion and all this anchored on Mutual Benefit for all at no additional cost to any of them, aidded by the secure Four-tier Eziit Peer-to-Peer Vetting and Approval System (EPPVAS).

Trust

Trust

The key influencer of cost in most short term future payment based transcations is lack of trust between the partys involved, which esssentially should be Individual buyers and Merchants. This forces both partys to engage in creditworthy or product reliability screening activities (which can be costly depending on the levels of lack of trust) to mitigate the possible risks such as default or substandard deliverables hence making the whole transaction more costly for both partys. However, Eziit creates a community in which trust is created and sustained consequently reducing on activities needed to maintain the respective relationship. This leads to low transaction costs hence both partys and/or other connected stakeholder such us employers and Financial Institutions if needed, gaining. This is made possible through the mandatory Eziit Peer-to-Peer Vetting and Approval System (EPPVAS) which creates reputation Collateral and compels good bevahour in fear of erroding the reputaion collateral, leading to being flagged as a high risk party to others both within and outside the Eziit Community.

Collaboration

Collaboration

Collaboration between and amongst stakeholders in any sector is usually benefial to all stakeholders if feasible. However, it is usually very difficult to collaborte in numbers big enough to unlock the full potential of such collaboration. This is usually due to conflicting interests and not having means to integrate all such interests. Eziit provides such integration of interests through creating a trust based community hence unlocks the mutual benefit embeded in collaboration, especially beyond physical lines.This collaboration created and sustained the mandatory Eziit Peer-to-Peer Vetting and Approval System (EPPVAS).

Mutual Benefit

Mutual Benefit

Mutual Benefits essentially speaks to sustainability. This is why Eziit is a mutual benefit centric platform which is created through establishing a trust based collaborative community as a means to creating sustainanble value for stakeholders, which in this case is Individuals, Employers , Merchants and Financial Institutions. This is ensured through the mandatory Four-tier Eziit Peer-to-Peer Vetting and Approval System (EPPVAS) which enables each stakeholder to endorse and monitor its interests while protecting those of others. EPPVAAS compels all in the Eziit community as it gives powers to add or reduce other's reputaion collateral based on how they rate them on each tranaction.

FOUR-TIER EZIIT PEER-TO-PEER VETTING & APPROVAL SYSTEM (EPPVAS)

EPPVAS

The Eziit Peer-to-Peer Vetting and Approval System (EPPVAS) refers to the multifaceted risk management system which ensures that Eziit users are properly vetted to the satisfaction of those they desire to do business with on the Eziit platform.

EPPVAS ensures that each party's interests are considered, confirmed and sustained as it anables stakeholders to vet the details of those they are doing business with and update when anything changes for their benefit and that of other Eziit users.

EPPVAS also ensures that users are engaged in real time and compeled to good business practices because whatever they can or cannot do through the Eziit platform is always through EPPVAS at the mercy of other stakeholders subject to their perception of their credibility.

FOUR-TIER EPPVAS

The Four-tier in the Eziit Peer-to-Peer Vetting and Approval System (EPPVAS) highlights how multifaceted the risk management system used on Eziit is, to ensure trust is established and most importantly, sustained. Therefore, the Four-tier phrase discribes the merging of technology and humans in terms of human stakeholders involved in the vetting process. This integration includes,

1. The Individual account holder,

2. The merchant,

3. Either the employer, Financial institution or both, and

4. The Eziit system,

The basic premise is to integrate technology and humans to achieve the most secure and efficient security system. This is because technology on its own can be too rigid hence not efficient while humans also on their own might end up missing out on certain things hence not secure enough. However, a combination of the two (02) brings about compliments which properly blend together secureness and efficiency to the benefit of the whole system.
