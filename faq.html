Questions from Individuals or Prospective Individual account holders based on the four (04) key Eziit stakeholders

Return to FAQs Menu

Individuals

Q1. So I will really not pay any interest on short term financing using Eziit?

ANS: No. You will not pay any Interest.

Q2. How sustainable is Eziit if no Interest is paid on Short Term loans?

ANS: Eziit is sustainable becuase it is anchored on collaboration for mutual gain hence all stakeholders have their benefits covered.

Employers

Q1. How can I transact if my employer is not yet on Eziit?

ANS: Just engage any Merchant (sole traders or even just your local kiosk) in your locality and have them sign up and start transacting with them on eziit to build your Reputation collateral.

Q2. How will payments be made if my Employer is not involved?

ANS: You will pay directly to the Merchant by cash or using any direct payment platforms available to you.

Merchnats

Q1. Can Merchants send a connection request to me offering their services?

ANS: Yes. Any Eziit User can initiate a connection request.

Q2. Can I also be registered as a Merchant while having an Eziit Individual account?

ANS: Yes. There are no restrictions to having a Merchant account.

Financial Institutions

Q1. So I cannot transact through Eziit without a Financial Institution Underwriting my transanctions?

ANS: You can, underwriting is optional depending on the requirments of specific Merchants

Q2. Can I have some transactions being underwriten while others are not?

ANS: Yes. You only use underwriting with Merchants who demand it from you.
