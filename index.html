What You Can Do With Your Individual Eziit Account

Eziit unclocks benefits embeded in short term finance for Individuals and this in turn has a trickledown effect which results in mutual benefits to other key stakeholders such as Employers, Merchants and Financial institutions.

01

Zero Interest (0%) Goods/Services Based Loans

Instead of getting High interest rate Short term loans to sort out unforeseen financial challenges, which essentially means paying for goods/services, you can through Eziit have access to goods/services and pay back within 30 days or any agreed upon period. This will make you Save as you pay exactly the amount for the goods/services gotten, nothing more, no interest to be paid.

02

Point of Sale Based Transactions

Pay for goods and services with your eziit App at the point of sale. You can either use the employer or Merchant approved amount (s). Just check if the shop/merchant is on your approved merchants list and you are good to go. The employer approved amount is paid through payroll while the merchant approved amount directly to the merchant by the account holder subject to the agreed unpo period

03

Reputaion Collateral Based Purchases

Get access to goods/services from a merchant who knows you personally or has approved a certain amount for you based on your good reputaion with them or other merchants. The key here is that your good reputaion acts as collateral to trust that you will pay within the agreed upon period.

04

Pay for Others

You can create a voucher for a certain amount and send to a third party to transact in any shop or with any merchant on your approved merchant list. The third party can be anywhere in the world and the voucher will still work as long as the voucher is presented to a shop or establishment on your approved merchnat list.

05

Group Purchases

You can create a group, add or invite others with eziit accounts and contribute for a payment. This can be used to contribute towards school fees for a sibling, contibute for a family event or settle any shared expenses.

06

Build Reputation Collateral

Each time you use eziit to pay for goods/services, and pay back within the agreed upon period, a record of this is kept and translated into reputaion collateral to give you more access to other merchants who will depend on this reputation to give you access to their goods/services. Therefore, each good act on eziit gives you more liverage in the eziit trust based community. This is because high cost future payment based business transactions are usually due to the related risk management costs hence if you are a low risk, then you should not pay for high risk managements costs and benefit from your good reputation.

2. Link Account (Details Below) 🡻

Individual

"Linking Individual Account" refers to Connecting your Individual Eziit acount to your Employer, Merchant or bothusing their Eziit Employer or Merchant account holder identification codes which are given to all Eziit account holders after opening an account and can be seen in their respective accounts after loging in.


This is the first step in activating your Eziit account and enables employers or merchants to verify your details and allocate an amount they will allow you to spend through eziit based on your net income for the employer or reputation for merchants.

Employer

"Linking Employer Account" refers to connecting your account as an Employer with merchants or Financial Institutions (optional)using their Eziit Codes. This is basically seeking permision from a merchant for your members of staff to access their goods/services.

It also servis as a commitment that you will deduct through your payroll and settle all outstanding payments within 30days or any agreed upon period. In the case of financial institutions, if needed in order for your staff to transact with certain merchants, it is basically asking if they could underwrite transactions for your members of staff.

Therefore, this is the first step in activating your Employer Eziit account and enables merchants and Financial institutions (if needed) to verify your details and agree to allow your staff access their goods/services for merchnats or underwrite transactions for financial institutions.

.

Merchant

"Linking Merchant Account" refers to connecting your Eziit Merchant account to Employers, individual Eziit acoount holders or financial institutions using their Eziit Codes found in all eziit account holders accounts.

This is basically offering your goods/services to members of staff of a respective employer to be paid for through the payroll wihin 30 days or an agreed upon period, or individuals with a good reputaion through past transactions with you or other trusted merchants.

Therefore, this is the first step in activating your Merchant Eziit account if no requests have been sent to you by other Eziit users and enables employers or individuals to verify your details and agree to do business with you. In the case of financial institutions, it is asking them if the could underwrite transactions with respective emlpoyers or individuals acccessing your goods/ services through eziit.

Financial Institution

"Linking Financial Institution Account" refers to connecting your Eziit Financial Institution account to Employers, individuals with Eziit acoounts or Merchants using their Eziit Codes. This is basically offering your services as an underwriter to Employers, individuals or Merchants.


Therefore, this is the first step in activating your Financial Institution Eziit account if no requests have been sent to you by other Eziit users and enables employers, Merchants or individuals to verify your details and agree to do business with you.

3. Approval (Details Below) 🡻

Individual

"Approval for individuals" basically refers to Individual Eziit account activation as one can only start using the account with other users who have accepted a connection or linking request from them.


Therefore, this is in a way accepting to conduct business with that individual for merchants or that individual doing business through that employer for employers.

This is a very important stage as it enables employers or merchants to own liability of what you do through your account subject to what has been verified by the respective employer or merchant.

The other approval is the optional request to financial intitutions to underwrite transactions if it is a mandatory requirement to do business with certain merchant for accounts not linked to any employer

Employer

"Approval for Employers" basically refers to Employer Eziit account activation as their members of staff can only start using their accounts with merchants who have accepted a connection or request from the employer.


Therefore, a Merchant accepting a request is in a way approving to conduct business with respective employers' members of staff.

This is a very important stage as it enables the employer to express their desire for their members of staff to access goods/services from the respective merchant and the merchant to attest to their willingness to do business with the members of staff of the requesting employer.

The other approval is the optional request to financial intitutions to underwrite transactions if it is a mandatory requirement to do business with certain merchants

Merchant

"Approval for Merchants" basically refers to merchant account activation as only members of staff for employers who have accepted a connection or request from the merchant can do business with the respective merchant, this is if the employer had not yet already sent a request to the merchant.


The other aspect could be for a merchant to send a resquest to individual account holders if they are comfortable with the respective individuals ability to settle their account with the merchant on time. Therefore, an employer or individual accepting a request is in a way approving to conduct business with the respective merchant.

This is a very important stage as it enables the merchant to express their desire to allow members of staff for a respective employer or an individual to access their goods/services and the employer or indidvidual to attest to their willingness to do business with the merchant and commitment to settle their accounts within the default 30 days or any agreed upon period.

The other approval is the optional request to financial intitutions to underwrite transactions if it is a mandatory requirement to do business with certain employers or individuals

Financial Institution

"Approval for financial institutions" basically refers to Financial Institution Eziit account activation by employers, individual account holders or merchants as underwrting eziit transactions is optional for all users subject to the requirements of those they would want to do business with.


Therefore, an employer, individual account holder or Merchant accepting a request is in a way approving to have the respective financial institution underwrite their transactions.

This is a very important stage as it enables the financial institution to express their desire to underwrite tranasctions for respective employers, individual account holders or merchants and these partys to attest to their willingness to have the aforementioned done for them by the financial institution.

4. Transact (Details Below) 🡻

Individual

"Transaction with the individual account" will include:

1. Pont of Sale payments through the Employer linked account,

2. Point of Sale Payments through the Merchant linked acocount,


3. Paying for Others (Sending Vouchers which others can use), and

4. Making Group payments (Contribution for shared expenses)

5. Sending account linking/connecting requests to employer or directly to merchant or Financial Institution,

6. Rating other users to contribute to their Eziit community reputaion collateral

Employer

"Transaction with the Employer account" will include:

1. Approving members of staff requests to link their account with the employer,

2. Send connection requests to Merchants and/or financial institutions,


3. Accepting connection requests from Merchants or Financial Institutions

4. Cancelling links for members of staff or connections with merchants and/or financial institutions

5. Rating other users connected with the respective employer and influence their reputation collateral

6. Make payments for goods and services accessed by members of staff during the due period.

Merchant

"Transaction" with the Merchant" account will include:

1. Download for free and be able to use the Eziit Merchant app for point of sale transactions


2. Approving members of staff requests to link their account with the merchant,

3. Send connection requests to employers, individuals and/or financial institutions,

4. Accepting connection requests from employers, individuals and/or Financial Institutions

5. Cancelling links for individuals, connections with employers and/or financial institutions

6. Rating other users connected with the respective merchant and influence their reputation collateral

7. Recieve payments for goods/services accessed by individuals from individuals (for accounts approve by merchant) and employers for accounts linked to employers during the due period.

Financial Institution

"Transaction with the Financial Institution" account will include:

1. Approving connection requests from employers, merchants or individuals to have their transactions underwritten,


2. Send connection requests to employers, individuals and/or merchants,

3. Accepting connection requests from employers, individuals and/or merchants

5. Cancelling links for individuals, connections with employers and/or merchants

7. Rating other users connected with the respective financial institution and influence their reputation collateral

8. Recieve payments from Eziit (the underwriting cost is embeded in the Transaction cost) for transactions underwritten for individuals from individuals accounts (for accounts approve by merchant) and employers for accounts linked to employers during the due period.
