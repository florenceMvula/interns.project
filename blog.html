The Short Term Debt Problem and the Eziit Solution

The continuous uptake of short-term consumer credit products whereby money is borrowed today and is paid back usually within one month with interest, as is the case with payday loans, salary advances, soft loans or whatever they are called for whatever reason, indicates strong market demand. This could be attributed to consumers not having alternatives when faced with short term finance needs, covinience or any other good reasons. However, such credit facilities are synonymous with high interest rates and evidence suggesting such credit leads to adverse consumer outcomes such as being trapped in debt traps and consumer financial distress due to high interest rates is prominent.

Eziit acknowleges that these high interest rates are becuase of real and perceived default risk considerations. Therefore, Eziit uses an integrated approach to develop a zero Interest (0%) Rate Model using an advanced Four-tier Ezitt Peer-to-Peer Vetting and Approval System (EPPVAS) to create a low risk trust based community centered on addressing information mismatch between partys in a transaction so that each party is prevented from witholding information which might be important for the other party to form an informed decsion (asymmetry of information). This is because the information constraint is the main cause of risk consequently high interest rates in short term credit transactions.

Why High Interest Rates are Charged on Short Term Loans

An Interest Rate is usually composed of four (04) elements which dictate whether the rate is high or low and these are,

1. Profit,
2. Provision for Non-Performing Loans (NPL),
3. Overheads (Outreach, Processing and General adminstrative costs), and
4. Cost of Funds used by the lender.

In a high risk environment, a bigger provision for NPLs and more outreach, processing and other administrative activities which all cost money will have to be undertaken making the interest high. Therefore, in a low risk environment, very little is spent on these activties making the overall interest rate low. What Eziit does using the Four-tier Eziit Peer-to-Peer Vetting and Approval System (EPPVAS) is to eradicate all these activties hence making a totally Zero Interest (0%)Rate for short term loans possible.

How Bad Debt affects everyone including those who don't Borrow

The preceeding section has indicated that high interest rates are usually due to high risk management activties such as provision for NPLs, processing, outreach and administrative activities which cost money.

Therefore, since risk is essentially a perception issue, if others getting loans are defaulting , the general risk perception of that society increases hence ultimately affecting the overall risk and this is what leads to everyone being affected because a component of the overal risk is usually considered as a whole (Systematic Risk) and factored in most products hence everyone being affected.

The irony is that there are fairly good records of defaults hence affecting the risk perception but alot of good practice goes unnoticed, especailly in the informal sector. Eziit through the Four-tier Eziit Peer-to-Peer Vetting and Aprroval System (EPPVAS) ensures both are done hence benefiting all stakeholders within the Eziit community.

How Everyone can Benefit frorm Zero Interest (0%) Short Term Loans

Eziit benefits everyone in the Eziit Community because it is a Trust based Mutual benefit community which enables Individuals to use Reputation as collateral for Zero interest (0%) short term loans, guarantees Employers increased productivity through stress free employees by increasing their net salaries at no extra cost to the employer, increases sales volume/profitability for Merchants and creates new low risk income streams for Financial Institutions.

The most important thing with eziit is that it pursues a sustainable solution because it focuses on mutual benefits which are protected by the Four-tier Eziit Peer-to-Peer Vetting and Approval System (EPPVAS).

Eziit General Zero Interest (0%) Short Term Loans Concept

The Eziit general Zero Interest (0%) Short term loans idea is anchored on unlocking the potential benefits embeded in short term consumer finance for Individuals, Employers of those who use short term finance, Merchants from whom users of short term finance buy goods/services from and Financial ainstitutions who either supply short term finance or could influence it, for mutual gain. The general premise is that the adverse effects of short term finance tend to ultimately affect all these stakeholders hence in the best interest of all these to be part of the solution.

These general adverse effects, which are intertwined among others range from, Individuals being trapped in debt traps, getting financially distress hence their productivity at work going down and the employer losing out. Being in a dept trap due to paying high interest on short term financing could mean having low take home salary and force individuals to start demanding for salary increments, not becuase the current salry was low but because most was being used to pay off high interest loans and this would lead to the employer losing out by increasing salaries or low productivity from a demotivated workforce.

On the part of Merchnats, a reduced net income for individuals means reduced purchasing power which could lead to buying less and less from the Merchants leading to relatively low sales volumes and profitability. In the case of Financial Institutions, most of their products are risk sensitive hence a general rise in the risk profile of individuals would make their products more expensive and end up initiating more financial related problems to the detriment of all stakeholders.

It is against this background that Eziit using the Four-tier Eziit Peer-to-Peer Vetting and Approval system (EPPVAS) creates a platform on which the need for actual money which is costly because of considerations for Profit, NPLs, Administrative costs and cost of money is erradicated by giving access to goods/services to individuals through the Eziit Product based short term loans.

Eziit Product Based Short Term Loans

Individuals who use short term consumer finance are never interested in the money they get and have to pay back with interest but the goods/services they can pay for using that money. If it was possible, a cheaper option would be to directly get the goods/services and pay at their payday. However, the trust between individuals and merchants is too low for such to happen, consequently they have to borrow money and immediatelly pay for goods/services. Therefore, the interest they pay back becomes the price for lack of trust and looking at statistics of how the short term consumer credit space has been growing in billions of dollars, that is too high a price to pay for lack of trust, especially if an alternative existed.

Eziit provises that alternative by creating a Eziit Trust based community in which Merchants could give access to their goods/services to individuals so that the money they would have instead paid out in the form of interest could continue circulating amongst themselves for mutual gain. This development which Eziit makes possible through its Four-tier Eziit Peer-to-Peer Vetting and Approval Systme (EPPVAS) is what is refered to as Zero Interest (0%) Product or Goods/services Based short term Loans.

In view of the foregoing, Eziit ensures that Trust is created and most importantly sustained through real time updates on all stakeholders so they can make informed decisions. This maintaining of trust through monitored good business practices then develops into a Reputaion of good business practice which can then be used as Collateral (Reputation Collateral) within the platform to have access to more opportunities to do business with more Eziit stakeholders.

Reputaion Collateral

Reputation Collateral simply means someone has a treceable good business practice of timely paying for things gotten on credit and not defaulting on busniess agreements hence being classified as a low risk party. This then is intented to attest to the good business practices of such a party and encourage others to do business with them or extend favourable credit faciilities without having to spend on vetting or other administrative costs to ensure they honor up to whatever they have pledged to do.

Eziit then ensures this Reputation Collateral is maintained by giving access to stakeholders to upadate on the conduct of the Party in question through the Four-tier Eziit Peer-to-Peer Vetting and Approval System (EPPVAS). This then compels Eziit users to good business practices as doing anything contrary could lead to erroding the Reputation Collateral and miss out on favourable business deals for them or could even be removed from the platform, a record kept forever and made available to all who inquire on doing business with them.
